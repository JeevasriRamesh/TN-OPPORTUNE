TNOpportune: A Personalized Eligibility-Based Job and Scheme Finder for Tamil Nadu.

TNOpportune offers an intelligent web-based platform designed to empower the citizens of Tamil Nadu by simplifying access to government job openings and welfare schemes. 
The system enables users to easily discover suitable opportunities based on their personal eligibility criteria, such as age, educational qualification, gender, caste category, and income level. 
By intelligently mapping user inputs to the eligibility parameters of various government programs and vacancies, TNOpportune eliminates the need for manual searching and confusion across multiple websites.
